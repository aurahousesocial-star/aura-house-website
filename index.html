OUR DEAL

Our Manifesto

Aura House was started by two friends tired of the digital first world and the isolation of the modern grind. We believe technology should serve the human experience, not replace it. Our goal is to use intentional curation to pull people out of their screens and back into the room for storytelling, nuance, and humor. Whether it's a game of chess or a drink at the bar, we're here to facilitate the one thing that makes us feel truly human: authentic community.

— Two friends who got fed up

WHO THIS IS FOR & who it ain't

✔ come in

  • you're tired of performing connection through a screen
  • you show up and put the phone away
  • you'd rather lose at chess than win at small talk
  • you think depth is more interesting than volume
  • you're here for the room, not the content

✘ stay out

  • you're networking in disguise
  • you photograph everything before you experience it
  • you can't sit with silence for five minutes
  • you're here to be seen, not to see anyone
  • you've already opened instagram twice reading this
